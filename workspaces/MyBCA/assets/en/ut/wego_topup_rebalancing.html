Rebalancing in Your Portfolio is Required due to:

  1. Movement in Market Conditions
    Changes in market conditions such as interest rates, capital market, regulations, political stability, and other factors may affect the performance of Mutual Fund products, causing your portfolio allocation to not be as targeted.
  2. Investment Transactions
    Investment transactions made such as subscriptions/auto-subscriptions, and redemption may also cause changes in your portfolio allocation.
  3. Other Conditions
    In addition to market condition movements and investment transactions, rebalancing may be required due to changes in strategy chosen, changes in BCA policy regarding allocation targets, as well as other factors.

What You Can Do:

Top up/Subscription
When you top up, the transaction amount will be allocated to certain asset class products, in order to achieve the asset allocation target.

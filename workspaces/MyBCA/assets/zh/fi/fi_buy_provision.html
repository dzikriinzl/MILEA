In the event that the Securities are deposited in a Sub Registry other than BCA Custodian, you shall instruct to such other Sub Registry to transfer the Securities into your Securities Account at BCA Custodian.

PURCHASE OF SECURITIES

  1. Before buying any Securities, you must first study and fully understand all information related to the relevant Securities including but not limited to the information contained in the Information Memorandum and/or Prospectus.
  2. You must provide BCA with an instruction to buy Securities either in writing or orally according to the Provisions applicable at BCA as well as other required documents (if any) to BCA before 15.00 Western Indonesia Time (WIB) on bourse day or at any other time as will be notified by BCA to Investors in any form and through any means.
  3. Any purchase transactions of Securities will be debited from your designated account pursuant to the rules applied by BCA.
  4. You shall provide an amount which is equal to the Total Proceeds of the Securities purchase transaction and other costs incurred in the purchase transactions (if any) on your designated account.
  5. Transaction Confirmation Letter for the purchase transactions of Securities will be delivered to you through any means in accordance with the prevailing laws.
  6. All taxes and fees arising in connection with the purchase transactions of Securities including but not limited to Stamp Duty on Transaction Confirmation Letter will be charged to you in accordance with the prevailing laws.
  7. BCA shall have the right to cancel any purchase of Securities if no adequate fund is available in your designated account and all losses incurred due to such cancellation of Securities purchase by BCA following the unavailability of adequate fund shall be fully borne by you. You hereby releases BCA from any claims, lawsuits, and/or other legal actions from any party including yourself.

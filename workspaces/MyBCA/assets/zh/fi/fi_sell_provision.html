  6. BCA has the right to charge a monthly Securities safekeeping fee, which will be debited directly from the fund account that you use for the purchase and/or sale of Securities through myBCA.
  7. You hereby authorize BCA to block and/or debit the fund account that you designate in the total amount of your obligations, which include among other things, the Total Proceeds and other fees charged to you in connection with the purchase and/or sale of Securities, including but not limited to the purchase fee, sale fee, and Securities safekeeping fee (if applicable) at BCA Custodian.

  9.   10. In order to conduct purchase and/or sale transactions of Securities with BCA, you must have a securities account at BCA Custodian. Furthermore, every purchase and/or sale transactions of Securities shall be conducted through your securities account at BCA Custodian.
  11. In the event that you intend to sell the Securities to BCA, the Securities proposed to be sold shall be available in your securities account at BCA Custodian at the time of the sale transaction. In the event that the Securities are deposited in a Sub Registry other than BCA Custodian, you shall instruct to such other Sub Registry to transfer the Securities into your Securities Account at BCA Custodian.
  12. You hereby instructs BCA that the total proceeds of the sale and/or redemption of the matured Securities shall be credited to your designated account.
  13. The authorization as referred to in points 7 and 9 above shall be irrevocable and/or non-cancelable and will not terminate for any reason including due to matters referred to in articles 1813, 1814 and 1816 of the Indonesian Civil Code until you have fulfill all your obligations arising in connection with the purchase and/or sale transactions of Securities.

SALE OF SECURITIES

  1. You must provide BCA with an instruction to sell Securities either in writing or orally according to the provisions applicable at BCA as well as other required documents (if any) to BCA before 15.00 Western Indonesia Time (WIB) on bourse day or at any other time as will be notified by BCA to Investors in any form and through any means.
  2. Any sale transactions of Securities will be debited directly from your designated account pursuant to the rules applied by BCA.
  3. The proceeds from the sale of Securities will be credited to your designate account. If you have not paid the Securities safekeeping fee at the time of the sale transaction, you hereby authorize BCA to debit your bank account for the purpose of paying the Securities safekeeping fee.
  4. Transaction Confirmation Letter for the sale transactions of Securities will be delivered to you through any means in accordance with the prevailing laws.
  5. All taxes and fees arising in connection with the sale transactions of Securities including but not limited to Stamp Duty on Transaction Confirmation Letter will be charged to you in accordance with the prevailing laws.
  6. In consideration of certain circumstances, BCA shall have the right to reject or delay sale transactions of Securities, including, but not limited to, when any one or more of the following events have occurred:
    1. the stock exchange at which the Securities traded is closed;
    2. trading of the Securities is delayed by the stock exchange or other relevant authority;
    3. states of emergency as set out in the Law Number 8 of 1995 on Capital Market, namely a force majeure the occurrence of which is beyond the capability of either party due to, among other things, war (including political or military turmoil), natural events such as earthquake or flood, strike, sabotage, or commotion (including social insurrection), a decrease in substantial part or all of the prices of the Securities listed on the stock exchange, so material and huge that a crash has occurred, or trading system fails or transaction settlement fails (including restriction of money changing, money transfer, or appropriation by the government);
    4. at the order of the relevant authority.
    Without prejudice to other provisions as specified in Terms and Conditions of Securities Purchase and/or Sale Transaction, the Securities may not be resold until the event causing such rejection or delay has ended in the opinion of BCA or at the order of the relevant authority.
